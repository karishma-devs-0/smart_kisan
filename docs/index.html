Privacy Policy for SmartKisan

Effective Date: May 23, 2026 · Last Updated: May 23, 2026

Hbeonlabs Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("we", "us", "our") operates the SmartKisan mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the rights you have over your data.

1. Who we are

Data Controller: Hbeonlabs Technologies Pvt. Ltd.
App: SmartKisan (package com.smartkisan.app)
Contact: support@smartkisan.app

2. Information we collect

Account information: email address (required), phone number (optional), name (optional), password (stored only as a one-way bcrypt hash — we cannot recover it), Google account identifier (only if you sign in with Google).

Farm and device data: farm name, type, size, and location coordinates you enter during onboarding; field names, soil types, sowing dates, crop varieties, photos you upload; pump configurations and run history; IoT device identifiers (MAC address, sensor type, battery level, last sync time); sensor readings (soil moisture, pH, temperature, NPK, EC) from your hardware.

Device permissions (granted by you when prompted):

  • Location (GPS): used to fetch local weather forecasts and recommend region-appropriate crops. We do not track your location continuously.
  • Camera & Photo Library: used to capture or select leaf photos for the AI Plant Disease Detection feature. Photos are sent for inference and not stored on our servers.
  • Push Notifications token: used to send alerts only if you enable notifications.
  • Network: required to communicate with our backend, MQTT broker, and third-party APIs.

Technical data: app version, device model, OS version, language preference (for crash diagnostics and feature compatibility); IP address (logged transiently by our backend for rate-limiting, not retained for analytics).

3. How we use your information

  • Authenticate you (login, session restore)
  • Run the App's farming features
  • Control your pumps in real time via MQTT
  • Show weather and crop recommendations
  • Perform AI plant disease detection on photos you submit
  • Send notifications you opt into
  • Improve App stability via crash reports (no personal data attached)

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

4. Who we share your information with

  • OpenWeatherMap: latitude/longitude only, to fetch weather forecasts.
  • HuggingFace Spaces: a single leaf photo at scan time for one-time disease detection inference, not retained.
  • MQTT broker: pump commands and sensor data scoped to your user ID.
  • Google Sign-In: only if you use it, we receive your email and Google sub identifier.
  • Cloud hosting provider: stores your account and farm data in a managed PostgreSQL database.

We do not sell your personal data to any party, ever. We do not share data with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Where your data is stored

Account, farm, and sensor data is stored in a managed PostgreSQL database hosted with our cloud provider. Photos for disease detection are sent to HuggingFace's servers and discarded after inference. MQTT messages are not persisted beyond delivery. All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS and WSS.

6. How long we keep your data

  • Account data: retained while active. Deleted within 30 days of an account deletion request.
  • Farm, crop, sensor data: retained while account is active. Deletable on request.
  • Plant disease scan photos: not retained.
  • Logs and crash reports: retained up to 90 days, then auto-purged.
  • MQTT message history: not retained.

7. Your rights

You can, at any time:

  • Access the data we hold about you — request via support@smartkisan.app
  • Correct inaccurate data via the in-app Profile screen
  • Delete your account and all associated data via Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Export your data in a machine-readable format on request
  • Withdraw consent for optional features via system permissions or in-app Settings
  • Opt out of any non-essential data collection

For users in jurisdictions with stronger data rights (EU/GDPR, India/DPDP Act), all rights granted by your local law apply.

8. Children's privacy

SmartKisan is intended for users 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Data security

  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt (cost factor 10); we never store them in plain text.
  • Session tokens are JWTs with a 30-day expiry.
  • All API traffic over HTTPS / TLS 1.2+.
  • Database access restricted to our backend server; not exposed publicly.

If we experience a data breach affecting your information, we will notify you within 72 hours of discovery.
